Three Steps to Perfect Vocals

Whether you start with a URL or a local file, the pipeline adapts automatically.

A
Download
yt-dlp fetches audio or video from any URL. Supports MP3, WAV, and video up to 1080p.

Skip this step with a local file.
B
Extract Audio
ffmpeg -q:a 0 -map a extracts the audio stream from any local video file at maximum quality. Pure audio files skip this step automatically.
C
Isolate Vocals
demucs -n htdemucs_ft --two-stems=vocals --shifts=2

Outputs vocals.wav and no_vocals.wav ‚ÄĒ zero music bleed.
